SF Beach

A wonderful place with amazing views. My family and I enjoyed watching families with children and pets enjoying in the cool waters. We walked along the shore with the waves lapping at our feet. It was a wonderful day!

Berry Picking

My family and I went berry picking this summer. We picked loads of strawberries, blackberries and boisenberries. It was a wonderful experience for both my sister and me, and I highly reccomend it.

Stow Lake Boating House

My family and I drove to SF to visit the Golden Gate Park where we went paddle boating at Stow Lake. The lake was very still apart from the ripples of the boats moving across the water. We also enjoyed a nice picnic in the park followed by ice cream!
